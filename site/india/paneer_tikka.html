Paneer Tikka

A classic Indian appetizer featuring marinated paneer cubes grilled to smoky perfection. The perfect blend of spice and char.

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup Thick Yogurt
  • 1 large Onion (in squares)
  • 1 large Bell Pepper (in squares)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tbsp Gram Flour (Besan)
  • 1.5 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Chaat Masala
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 2 tbsp Mustard Oil
  • Salt to taste
Prep: 20 min | Marinate: 2 hrs | Cook: 15 min

Instructions

  1. Create a thick marinade by whisking yogurt with all spices, gram flour, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and salt.
  2. Heat mustard oil until lightly smoking, cool slightly, and mix into the marinade.
  3. Gently coat paneer, onion, and bell pepper cubes in the marinade. Refrigerate and let marinate for at least 2 hours.
  4. Preheat oven to 220¬∞C (430¬∞F). Thread the marinated items onto skewers.
  5. Arrange skewers on a baking tray and bake for 10-15 minutes, turning once, until charred at the edges.
  6. Sprinkle with chaat masala and serve hot with mint chutney.
